We were born on the web.

We know our way around

We have been building accessible web solutions since the Internet was just a baby. We're proud of making websites that work, but we don’t just implement. We strategize, plan, design, build, and maintain the entire lifecycle of your web channel.

Our process

All our projects go through our patented (okay, not exactly patented) Yellow Pencil 4D Process: discovery, design, development, and deployment. Visit responsiveprocess.com to learn about the nitty gritty of our process. Here’s a snapshot of what you can expect while we work, and work... and work.

1. Discovery

Like a first date, but better. First we ask you a whole bunch of (important) questions. Then we set goals, decide how the project will unfold, and who will be involved.

  • Content audit
  • Brand and identity audit
  • Business analysis
  • Technical audit
  • User research
  • Project planning
  • Search strategy

2. Design

Good design depends on user experience. This is where pencil hits paper and we make sure your project will look, feel, read, and function like it should.

  • Branding and identity
  • Interaction design
  • Information architecture
  • Content strategy
  • Visual design
  • SEO
  • Social media planning

3. Development

This is where beauty and utility meet. We dig deep into pixels and code and build the real thing – no matter how much blood, sweat, or coffee it takes.

  • CMS development – specializing in OpenText and Drupal
  • Custom apps
  • Accessibility standards – W3C, WCAG
  • Browser and device testing
  • Social media integration
  • e-Commerce

4. Deployment

We make sure that your project is properly launched and ready for the long haul by giving you the plans and tools to maintain it.

  • Content migration
  • Hosted solutions
  • SaaS (Software as a Service)
  • SLA (Service Level Agreements)
  • Training and documentation
  • Governance planning
  • User Acceptance Testing
  • Boisterous launch parties
